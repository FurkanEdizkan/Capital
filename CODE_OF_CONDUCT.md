# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the Capital project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the project

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
and publishing others' private information without permission.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — the repository,
issues, pull requests and discussions.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the
project maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly
and fairly.
